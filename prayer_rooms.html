About Us

The Muslim Student Association of Cal State Fullerton's main purpose is to educate the CSUF community about Islam, and represent it accurately. We also aim to be active and friendly with the CSUF community and establish relationships with various student organizations.
The MSA stands for justice and peace by being vocal on major issues throughout the world. The MSA is also a place for Muslims on campus to call home. It strives to strengthen the bond between Muslims regardless of background.
